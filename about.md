with [HoloViz](https://holoviz.org) under the
[NumFOCUS](https://numfocus.org) umbrella. My project is the **Lumen
and xarray integration** (350 hours). My mentors are Andrew Huang and
Andy Maloney.

This blog is the biweekly progress log NumFOCUS asks every GSoC
contributor to keep. The plan is short, honest notes every two weeks
between now and end of August: what worked, what did not, what I
changed my mind about.
